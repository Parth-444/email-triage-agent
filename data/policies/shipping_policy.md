# Mumzworld Shipping Policy

Mumzworld delivers across the UAE, KSA, and other GCC destinations with shipping options designed to keep things simple and predictable for families. Delivery timelines begin once the order is confirmed and prepared for dispatch.

For orders delivered within the UAE, standard delivery usually takes 1 to 3 business days. Orders above 100 AED qualify for free standard shipping. Orders below that threshold may incur a delivery fee shown at checkout. Customers in the UAE may also choose express shipping for next-day delivery, subject to service coverage and order cut-off times, for an additional 30 AED.

For Saudi Arabia, standard delivery typically takes 3 to 5 business days. A flat shipping rate of 25 SAR applies unless a promotion states otherwise. Cash on delivery is available in KSA for eligible orders and locations.

For other GCC countries, delivery generally takes 5 to 7 business days depending on destination, customs processing, and courier network coverage. Final shipping fees and available payment methods are displayed during checkout before payment is completed.

Cash on delivery is available in the UAE and KSA on eligible orders. We also offer secure prepaid payment options through major cards and selected digital payment methods shown at checkout.

Once your order ships, tracking updates are sent by email and, where available, by SMS. These updates include the latest shipment progress and help you follow your parcel through to delivery. If there is any delay, our support team will do their best to assist with the latest available courier information.
