# Mumzworld FAQ

## 1. What payment methods do you accept?
We accept major credit and debit cards, selected digital payment options, and cash on delivery for eligible UAE and KSA orders.

## 2. Do you offer loyalty points?
Promotional reward programs may be available during selected campaigns. Any active points or rewards offer will be shown clearly on the website or app.

## 3. Is gift wrapping available?
Gift wrapping is available on selected items. If the option appears at checkout, you can add it before placing your order.

## 4. Do you have physical store locations?
Mumzworld primarily operates as an online shopping platform. If pop-up events or partner activations are available, they will be announced through official channels.

## 5. Where can I download the Mumzworld app?
The Mumzworld app is available through the Apple App Store and Google Play Store, subject to your region.

## 6. Do you provide size guides?
Yes. Many clothing, footwear, and gear listings include size or dimension guidance on the product page.

## 7. Are your products authentic?
Yes. We source products from authorized suppliers and trusted brand partners.

## 8. How can I cancel an order?
If your order has not shipped yet, please contact support as soon as possible with your order number to request cancellation.

## 9. Can I change my delivery address after placing an order?
Address changes may be possible before dispatch. Please contact support quickly and we will confirm whether the shipment can still be updated.

## 10. Do you support bulk orders?
Yes. For large quantity purchases, please contact customer support with the product list and required quantities.

## 11. Do you offer corporate gifting?
Yes. Corporate gifting and curated bundles may be arranged through the support team depending on availability and timelines.

## 12. How can I subscribe to the newsletter?
You can subscribe from the website footer or through account preferences when available.

## 13. How can I contact customer support?
You can contact support through the help channels listed on the website or app, including email and contact forms where available.

## 14. What are your customer support working hours?
Support operating hours may vary by season and campaign period. For the latest schedule, please check the official help page.

## 15. Do products come with a warranty?
Warranty availability depends on the brand and product type. If a warranty applies, the relevant details are usually noted on the product page or packaging.
