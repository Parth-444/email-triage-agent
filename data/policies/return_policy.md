# Mumzworld Return Policy

At Mumzworld, we want you to shop with confidence. If something is not quite right, most items may be returned within 15 days from the delivery date as long as they are unused, in their original packaging, and in resalable condition with all tags, manuals, and accessories included. Returns requested after the applicable window may not be approved, but our team will still review exceptional cases and advise on possible alternatives such as store credit or manufacturer support.

For baby monitors, electronic devices, and similar electronic items, the return window is 7 days from delivery. These items must be returned complete, undamaged, and with all original packaging. If the product arrives damaged, defective, or not as described, please contact us as soon as possible so we can arrange an immediate replacement or a refund after verification.

For hygiene and consumable categories, opened items cannot be returned. This includes baby formula, opened diaper packs, opened wipes bundles sold as sealed hygiene goods, and selected personal care products where hygiene rules apply. Unopened items from these categories may be reviewed according to the standard return policy if they remain sealed and in sellable condition.

If your return is approved, you may choose one of the following options depending on the item and order status:

- Refund to the original payment method
- Exchange for another size, color, or similar item where available
- Store credit valid for 12 months from the date of issue

Refunds to the original payment method are typically processed within 5 to 7 business days after the returned item passes inspection. Depending on your bank or card issuer, the final posting date may vary. Exchanges are processed subject to stock availability. If an exchange item is unavailable, we may offer a refund or store credit instead.

Items that are damaged, defective, or incomplete on arrival are handled with priority. In such cases, our team may arrange an immediate replacement or a full refund once the issue is confirmed. To help us resolve these requests quickly, please share your order number and clear photos of the issue when possible.

If you are unsure whether your item is eligible, our customer care team will be happy to guide you. We always aim to offer a fair and practical solution for families shopping with Mumzworld.
