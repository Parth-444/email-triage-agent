# Available Skills

- **return_request**: Handle return, refund, exchange, or damaged item emails. Load when customer wants to return or replace a product.
- **order_inquiry**: Handle "where's my order" and delivery tracking emails. Load when customer asks about order status.
- **product_question**: Handle product availability, recommendations, and info requests. Load when customer asks about a specific product or wants suggestions.
- **complaint_escalation**: Handle angry, threatening, or repeated-issue emails. Load when urgency is high/critical or customer is aggressive.
- **general_inquiry**: Handle shipping, payment, policy, and general questions. Load when the question is about Mumzworld policies or operations.
